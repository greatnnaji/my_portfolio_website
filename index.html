I'm a full-stack developer working at the intersection of traditional software and AI. I've always needed to know why things work. As a kid, I couldn't just use a website, I had to understand what made it tick.

My work ethic comes from an unlikely place: delivering newspapers through Manitoba winters. Those Thursday evenings taught me that showing up isn't negotiable, especially when your team depends on you.

At Scaled Analytics, I built automation scripts that centralized regression testing, helping the team catch bugs faster and cut down manual work. What draws me to AI and ML is how it levels the playing field for anyone curious enough to use it. I love working on systems where you can see exactly why a feature matters, whether I'm debugging a complex issue or integrating LangChain and LLM APIs.

I structure my life around discipline. When I commit to something, I finish it.
