Learn About Me

Curiosity, discipline, and building systems that matter

I'm a full-stack developer exploring the intersection of traditional software and AI. I've always been the kind of person who needs to know why. As a kid, I couldn't just use a website, I needed to understand what made it work. That curiosity led me to code, but my work ethic came from delivering newspapers in harsh Manitoba winters. Those Thursday evenings taught me that discipline isn't optional; it's how you show up for your team.

What excites me about AI and ML is how it levels the playing field for anyone curious enough to use it. At Scaled Analytics, I built automation scripts that centralized regression testing, helping the team catch bugs faster and reduce manual work. I love working on systems that affect real people, where you can see exactly why a feature matters. Whether I'm debugging a complex issue or integrating LangChain and LLM APIs, I lose track of time figuring out how things fit together.

When I'm not coding, I'm reading books like Never Eat Alone or playing soccer, a sport I wasn't always good at, but one that proved hard work gets you there. I structure my life around discipline, and when I commit to something, I do everything in my power to finish it.
